# <a name="invoice-subscription-transactions"></a><span data-ttu-id="5c3a9-103">Ylläpitosopimustapahtumien laskuttaminen</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c3a9-103">Invoice subscription transactions</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]



1.  <span data-ttu-id="5c3a9-104">Valitse **Huoltohallinta** \> **Yleinen** \> **Huoltotilaukset** \> **Kaikki huoltotilaukset**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c3a9-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service subscriptions** \> **All service subscriptions**.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="5c3a9-105">Valitse huollon ylläpitosopimus ja napsauta **Muokkaa** tai luo huoltotilaus napsauttamalla **Ylläpitosopimus**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c3a9-105">Select a service subscription and then click **Edit** or click **Subscription** to create a service subscription.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="5c3a9-106">Valitse **Analysoi** \> **Maksutapahtumat**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c3a9-106">Click **Analyze** \> **Fee transactions**.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="5c3a9-107">Valitse laskutettava maksutapahtuma tai laskutettavat maksutapahtumat ja valitse sitten **Toiminnot** \> **Laskutusehdotus** avataksesi **Laskuehdotukset**-lomakkeen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c3a9-107">Select the fee transaction or fee transactions to invoice, and then click **Functions** \> **Invoice proposal** to open the **Invoice proposals** form.</span></span>

5.  <span data-ttu-id="5c3a9-108">Valitse **Luo lasku** ja anna tarvittavat parametrit **Luo lasku** -lomakkeessa</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c3a9-108">Click **Create invoice** and enter the appropriate parameters in the **Create invoice** form</span></span>

6.  <span data-ttu-id="5c3a9-109">Luo laskuehdotus napsauttamalla **OK** **Laskuehdotukset**-lomakkeessa.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c3a9-109">Click **OK** to create the invoice proposal in the **Invoice proposals** form.</span></span>

7.  <span data-ttu-id="5c3a9-110">Tarkista laskun sisältö, napsauta **Kirjaa lasku** avataksesi **Kirjaa lasku** -lomakkeen ja kirjataksesi laskun.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c3a9-110">Verify the contents of the invoice, click **Post invoice** to open the **Post invoice** form to post the invoice.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="5c3a9-111">Voit myös tulostaa laskun **Kirjaa lasku** -lomakkeesta.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="5c3a9-111">You can also print the invoice from the **Post invoice** form.</span></span>
