# <a name="delete-a-service-bom"></a><span data-ttu-id="e1cad-103">Huoltotuoterakenteen poistaminen</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1cad-103">Delete a service BOM</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="e1cad-104">Valitse **Palvelunhallinta** \> **Yleinen** \> **Palvelusopimukset** \> **Palvelusopimukset**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1cad-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service agreements**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e1cad-105">Valitse huoltosopimus josta haluat poistaa huollon tuoterakenteen (BOM).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1cad-105">Select the service agreement that you want to delete a service bill of materials (BOM) from.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="e1cad-106">Valitse **toimintoruudussa** **Huoltokohteet**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1cad-106">On the **Action Pane**, click **Service objects**.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="e1cad-107">Valitse **Huoltokohteet**-lomakkeessa huoltokohde josta haluat poistaa huollon tuoterakenteen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1cad-107">In the **Service objects** form, select the service object that you want to delete a service BOM from.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e1cad-108">Napsauta **Toiminnot** ja valitse **Poista huollon tuoterakenne**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1cad-108">Click **Functions**, and then select **Delete service BOM**.</span></span>

4.  <span data-ttu-id="e1cad-109">Valitse **Kyllä**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1cad-109">Click **Yes**.</span></span>


> [!NOTE]
> <P><span data-ttu-id="e1cad-110">Jos et ole käyttänyt mallituoterakennetta, huollon tuoterakenteen voi poistaa <STRONG>Mallituoterakenteet</STRONG>-lomakkeelta.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1cad-110">If you have not applied a template BOM, the service BOM can be deleted from the <STRONG>Template BOMs</STRONG> form.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="e1cad-111">Jos olet kuitenkin käyttänyt mallia huollon tuoterakenteeseen, sitä ei voi poistaa tällä menetelmällä.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="e1cad-111">If you have applied a template BOM, the service BOM cannot be deleted by using this method.</span></span></P>
