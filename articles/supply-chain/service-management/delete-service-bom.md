# <a name="delete-a-service-bom"></a><span data-ttu-id="45ea6-103">Huoltotuoterakenteen poistaminen</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45ea6-103">Delete a service BOM</span></span> 

[!include [banner](../includes/banner.md)]


1.  <span data-ttu-id="45ea6-104">Valitse **Palvelunhallinta** \> **Yleinen** \> **Palvelusopimukset** \> **Palvelusopimukset**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45ea6-104">Click **Service management** \> **Common** \> **Service agreements** \> **Service agreements**.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="45ea6-105">Valitse huoltosopimus josta haluat poistaa huollon tuoterakenteen (BOM).</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45ea6-105">Select the service agreement that you want to delete a service bill of materials (BOM) from.</span></span>

2.  <span data-ttu-id="45ea6-106">Valitse **toimintoruudussa** **Huoltokohteet**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45ea6-106">On the **Action Pane**, click **Service objects**.</span></span>

3.  <span data-ttu-id="45ea6-107">Valitse **Huoltokohteet**-lomakkeessa huoltokohde josta haluat poistaa huollon tuoterakenteen.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45ea6-107">In the **Service objects** form, select the service object that you want to delete a service BOM from.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="45ea6-108">Napsauta **Toiminnot** ja valitse **Poista huollon tuoterakenne**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="45ea6-108">Click **Functions**, and then select **Delete service BOM**.</span></span>
