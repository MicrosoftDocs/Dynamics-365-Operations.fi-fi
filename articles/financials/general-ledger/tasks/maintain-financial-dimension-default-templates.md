1. <span data-ttu-id="ab8dd-106">Valitse siirtymisruudussa **Moduulit > Kirjanpito > Tilikartta > Dimensiot > Taloushallinnon dimensioiden oletusmallit**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ab8dd-106">In the navigation pane, go to **Modules > General ledger > Chart of accounts > Dimensions > Financial dimension default templates**.</span></span>

9. <span data-ttu-id="ab8dd-115">Valitse **Lisää**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ab8dd-115">Select **Add**.</span></span>
