<span data-ttu-id="bc3e7-105">Kassatilanne on kassavirran ennuste.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bc3e7-105">Cash position is the projection of cash flow that is forecast for the near term.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="bc3e7-106">Se perustuu kassakuittien ennusteeseen niiltä asiakkailta, jotka maksavat maksamattomia laskuja ja tilauksia ja myös ennusteen käteissuorituksiin, jotka on maksettu toimittajille ostolaskuja ja -tilauksia varten.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="bc3e7-106">It's based on the projection of cash receipts from customers that pay outstanding invoices and orders, and also on the projection cash disbursements that are paid to vendors for purchase invoices and orders.</span></span>
